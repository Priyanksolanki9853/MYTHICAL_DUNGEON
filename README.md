## ‚öîÔ∏è About The Game
**Nanobot's Enigma** is a 3D Action RPG developed in Unity. 

Players explore a stylized, "techno-dungeon" open world, battling mechanical enemies and uncovering secrets. The game combines hack-and-slash combat with exploration elements, featuring high-fidelity lighting and custom assets created in Blender.

Currently, the game features **2 fully playable levels** with distinct environmental storytelling.

## ‚ú® Key Features
* **Action-RPG Combat:** Engage in strategic battles against various enemy types.
    * *Melee Bots:* Close-range mechanical spiders and warriors.
    * *Ranged Turrets:* Long-distance energy shooters that require dodging.
* **Exploration Loop:** Navigate the open world to find paths, collect **Health Packs** for survival, and gather **Coins** to track your score.
* **Live Animated Menu:** A fully animated 3D main menu scene with dynamic camera movements and lighting.
* **High-Quality Visuals:**
    * Custom 3D models modeled in **Blender**.
    * Atmospheric lighting and post-processing effects.
    * Immersive sound effects for combat and ambience.
